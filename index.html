Undergraduate Research Assistant

May 2023 - PRESENT

School of Computer Science | University of Oklahoma

My research proposal focuses on using reinforcement learning to remove chromatic aberrations in optical devices. Chromatic aberration, which affects image quality, is a common issue in cameras, telescopes, and microscopes. By transforming the optical device into a virtual game environment, an AI agent will learn to optimize image quality by manipulating variables like lens types and configurations. Through collecting simulation-based and experimental data, statistical analysis and visualization techniques will be applied to identify effective lens configurations. The expected outcome is an optimized setting that minimizes chromatic aberrations, benefiting fields like photography and medical imaging. This research aims to contribute to advancements in optics and image processing through the application of cutting-edge techniques.
